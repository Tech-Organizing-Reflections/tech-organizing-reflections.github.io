From Tech Lash to Tech Fash: Strategic Reflections on a Decade of Collective Organizing in Computing

Call for Participation People

Workshop Overview

This workshop brings together organizers, researchers, and tech workers to reflect on ten years of collective action in computing. We will examine key events, organizing strategies, and movement-building lessons across academia, industry, and civil society.

Themes

Topics include the techlash moment, labor organizing in Big Tech, abolitionist and anti-colonial tech politics, strategic refusals, and possibilities for future coalition-building within and beyond computing.
